Meet Your Instructor

Hi, I'm James Rumble

I've been playing guitar for over 20 years. My passion for music spans across a variety of styles — from rock and metal in my younger years, to blues, funk, pop, and classical music.

Currently, I perform with White Noise, a professional wedding and function band. You can check us out at whitenoiseband.uk and on Instagram.

Alongside performing, I’ve spent over 2 years teaching guitar to students of all ages, from young children in an extracurricular program designed to introduce them to playing in a band, to adults looking to refine their skills. I also work for a music trust teaching music in schools around Kent during the day time.

I believe that learning music is a journey for everyone, regardless of age or experience, and I’m passionate about helping students achieve their goals.

✅ Fully DBS Checked 📍 Based in Caterham, Surrey

The Approach

A Simple Framework

1. Personalised to You

We choose music, genres, and exercises that match your specific musical tastes and preferred learning style.

2. Making it Rewarding

The goal is to eliminate frustration. We break songs down into bite-sized, achievable pieces so you see progress fast.

3. Ongoing Support

You're never stuck on your own. Drop me an email or a video clip during the week if you need a quick tip on your posture or a riff.
